Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2025

律疏（以下简称「本应用」）尊重并保护用户隐私。本政策说明本应用如何处理您的数据。

一、我们不收集的数据

本应用不收集、不存储、不上传任何个人身份信息。我们没有服务器，不建立用户账号，不追踪您的使用行为。

二、本地存储的数据

以下数据仅存储于您的设备本地或您的个人iCloud账户中，我们无法访问：

· API Key：您配置的DeepSeek API Key加密存储于系统Keychain，可选同步至您的iCloud Keychain。

· 阅读记录：您的阅读位置和历史存储于iCloud Key-Value Store，仅您本人可访问。

· 对话记录：AI咨询的对话内容存储于iCloud Documents，仅您本人可访问。

· 应用设置：界面偏好等设置存储于本地UserDefaults。

三、AI咨询功能与第三方服务

使用AI法律顾问功能时，您的问题将直接从您的设备发送至DeepSeek（deepseek.com）的API接口。律疏不对该数据做任何中转、存储或分析。DeepSeek的数据处理方式请参阅其隐私政策。

四、法律数据

应用内置的法律法规数据来源于国家法律法规数据库，属于公开政府文件。这些数据完整存储于您的设备本地，无需联网即可访问。

五、儿童隐私

本应用不面向13岁以下儿童，不会故意收集儿童个人信息。

六、联系我们

如对本隐私政策有任何疑问，请通过以下方式联系：

邮箱：classicalxie@163.com

Lvshu (hereinafter referred to as "this app") respects and protects user privacy. This policy explains how we handle your data.

1. Data We Do NOT Collect

This app does not collect, store, or upload any personally identifiable information. We operate no servers, create no user accounts, and track no user behavior.

2. Locally Stored Data

The following data is stored only on your device or in your personal iCloud account, and is inaccessible to us:

· API Key: Your DeepSeek API Key is encrypted and stored in the system Keychain, with optional iCloud Keychain sync.

· Reading History: Your reading position and history are stored in iCloud Key-Value Store, accessible only to you.

· Chat History: AI consultation conversations are stored in iCloud Documents, accessible only to you.

· App Settings: Interface preferences are stored in local UserDefaults.

3. AI Consultation & Third-Party Services

When using the AI legal advisor feature, your questions are sent directly from your device to DeepSeek's API. Lvshu does not intermediate, store, or analyze this data. Please refer to DeepSeek's privacy policy for their data handling practices.

4. Legal Data

The built-in legal database is sourced from the National Laws and Regulations Database, comprising public government documents. This data is stored entirely on your device and accessible offline.

5. Children's Privacy

This app is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at:

Email: classicalxie@163.com
