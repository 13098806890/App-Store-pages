常见问题

AI法律顾问功能为什么无法使用？

AI法律顾问功能需要配置DeepSeek API Key。请进入「设置」页面，在「DeepSeek API Key」处粘贴您的Key并点击「验证并保存」。DeepSeek新用户注册即有免费额度，价格低廉，国内可直连。

如何获取DeepSeek API Key？

访问 platform.deepseek.com，注册账号后在「API Keys」页面创建即可。新用户注册后有免费额度，无需付费即可体验。

我的API Key安全吗？

您的API Key仅加密存储于本机系统Keychain中（可选择同步至iCloud Keychain），不会上传至任何第三方服务器。AI咨询时您的问题直接发送至DeepSeek，律疏不做任何中转或存储。

法律数据需要联网吗？

不需要。1526部法律法规数据完整内置于App中，无需网络即可浏览全部法条和进行全文检索。仅AI咨询功能需要联网调用DeepSeek接口。

法律数据多久更新一次？

数据来源于国家法律法规数据库，收录截至App发布时的现行有效法律法规。更新将随App版本更新发布。

iCloud同步不工作怎么办？

请确认设备已登录iCloud账号，并在「系统设置 → Apple ID → iCloud」中确认「iCloud云盘」已开启。阅读进度和对话记录的同步依赖iCloud服务。

搜索时为什么找不到内容？

全文搜索需要至少输入3个字符才能触发条文内容搜索。建议使用具体的法律术语，例如「合同解除」「劳动报酬」等。也可在设置中关闭「仅搜索法律标题」以搜索条文内容。

FAQ

Why can't I use the AI Legal Advisor?

The AI Legal Advisor requires a DeepSeek API Key. Go to Settings and paste your Key in the "DeepSeek API Key" field, then tap "Verify & Save". New DeepSeek users receive free credits to get started.

How do I get a DeepSeek API Key?

Visit platform.deepseek.com, create an account, and generate a Key in the "API Keys" page. New users receive free credits with no upfront payment required.

Is my API Key safe?

Your API Key is encrypted and stored in the system Keychain (with optional iCloud Keychain sync). It is never uploaded to any third-party server. When you use AI consultation, your questions are sent directly to DeepSeek without intermediation or storage by Lvshu.

Does the legal database require internet access?

No. All 1,526 laws and regulations are bundled within the app and work fully offline for browsing and full-text search. Only the AI consultation feature requires internet access to call DeepSeek's API.

How often is the legal data updated?

The data is sourced from the National Laws and Regulations Database, covering legislation effective as of the app's release. Updates are delivered through app version updates.

iCloud sync isn't working — what should I do?

Make sure your device is signed into iCloud and that "iCloud Drive" is enabled in Settings → Apple ID → iCloud. Reading progress and chat history sync depend on iCloud services.

Why can't I find what I'm searching for?

Full-text search requires at least 3 characters. Try using specific legal terminology such as "contract termination" or "labor compensation". You can also disable "Search titles only" in Settings to search within article content.
